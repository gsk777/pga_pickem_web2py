5 Total Events:

  • The Players Championship (March 10-13)
  • The Masters (April 7-10)
  • PGA Championship (May 19-22)
  • US Open (June 16-19)
  • British Open (July 14-17)

Each participant selects FIVE players for each event.

Picks are restricted based on the Official World Golf Ranking (OWGR) as follows:

  • #1-10 OWGR *Top-10 pick must be different in each of the five events*
  • #11-50 OWGR
  • #11-50 OWGR
  • #51+ OWGR
  • #51+ OWGR

Submitting Picks

  • Picks will be accepted as soon as the field becomes available (usually sometime on Monday evening or Tuesday morning of the event).
  • Picks are submitted through the event link on the SCHEDULE page.
  • Picks must be submitted by Midnight PST the night before the event.
  • In the event that no picks are submitted on time, selections will be randomly generated.

Top-10 Picks

  • Must be different in each of the five events.
  • Previously selected Top-10 picks can be found in your PROFILE.
  • If an already selected Top-10 player drops out of the Top 10, they may be selected again.

Withdrawals

  • If a selected player withdraws from the event BEFORE they start their 1st round, a replacement pick can be submitted via email to Graham. If no replacement is submitted, a replacement will be randomly generated.
  • If a selected player withdraws from the event AFTER they started their 1st round, no replacement pick can be made and they will receive 0 points.

Pick Sheet

  • When the tournament begins, a pick sheet with everyone's selections can be viewed via the SCHEDULE page.
